Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Why i am removed from room?

You must have an id with level more then 40, headshot percent not more then 28 and minimum 50 matches played in most of seasons to play BattleOP Gaming tournaments and the mentioned map (sanhok, miramar, vikendi) also needed to be downloaded to join any tournaments.

How to get room id and password?

Room ID and Password (for Esports Mobile, Free Fire, etc. ) are shared inside the app. Just open the app and scroll down to the match you participated, click on it and there you will get the Room ID and Password before 6-8 minutes of match start time.

How to find my Esports username and character id?
Click in your Esports profile pic. On upper side of your profile pic you will see 9-10 digit code which is your character id and below that you will find your Esports username.

Can i team up with my friends in room?
Yes, you can team-up and play with your friends in a same team in a match. To do so, make sure you and your friends have registered for the same match and when the Room ID and Password are shared, make sure all your registered friends and you join the room and take the same team slot.
How to do multiple enteries in a match?
You can register more than 1 entry from same BattleOP account. To do so, first register one entry in a match and then you will get option to add more entries to the same match. Use this option (+ Join) to add more entries. This way you can register multiple entries one by one in to same match.
How to cancel an entry?
BattleOP does provide an option to cancel entry from any match and get refund. But this option has to be used before 60 minutes of Match Start time only. To cancel your entry, just follow the steps below. Open App and Select the Game (Esports Mobile, Free Fire, etc.). Click on the Match you have joined in which you want to cancel the entry. You will be in the Match Details Screen. At the bottom left side, you will see MY ENTRIES Option. Click on it and your entries to that match will be listed. Click on the ( ) option beside the entry you want to delete. You get instant refund for the match entry in your BattleOP Wallet.
How to edit registered in-game name and character id?
Open App and Select the Game (Esports Mobile, Free Fire, etc.). Click on the Match you have joined in which you want to edit the entry. You will be in the Match Details Screen. At the bottom left side, you will see MY ENTRIES Option. Click on it and your entries to that match will be listed. Click on the pencil or edit icon beside your entry you want to Edit. In the pop-up, you can edit your In-Game Name and ID. Once done, Click on SUBMIT button and that’s it. You can now play with your updated changes.
Why my account is suspended?
If your account is suspended then that means you were found violating the terms and conditions or the gameplay rules. In such cases, the account won’t be recovered and all the balance in it is lost. Kindly make sure you strictly follow the fair play policies and gameplay rules. Thank you!
